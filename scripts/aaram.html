AARAM 25: A Grand Celebration of Creativity and Innovation

Thiruvananthapuram, March 2: The B.Arch Department of College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET) successfully hosted ‘Aaram 25’, its highly anticipated technical fest, on February 28 and March 1. The event was a vibrant blend of architecture, art, and entertainment, drawing enthusiastic participation from students across Kerala. The fest featured a variety of attractions, including book stalls, group games, and an exhibition showcasing the department's finest works. Visitors were captivated by the wall posters, stickers, and other creative merchandise on display. The face painting stall was a crowd favorite, adding a fun and artistic touch to the celebrations. One of the key highlights of Aaram 25 was the Film Conclave, held on the final day, where cinema enthusiasts engaged in insightful discussions. Adding to the fest’s charm was the Gazal Night by Ashbel Peter on February 28, which left the audience mesmerized with soulful melodies. The fest also featured exclusive Aaram 25 T-shirts, which were available for sale in vibrant colors, becoming a cherished souvenir for attendees. With students from various colleges across Kerala joining in, Aaram 25 was a resounding success, celebrating creativity, technical excellence, and cultural exchange in an unforgettable manner.
