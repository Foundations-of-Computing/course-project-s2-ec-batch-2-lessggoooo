CET Dominates A-Zone Inter-College Cricket Tournament, Clinches Title in Style

Trivandrum, January 30, 2025 – The A-Zone Inter-College Cricket Tournament, conducted by APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) at the Medical College Ground, Trivandrum, saw an undisputed champion emerge—College of Engineering, Trivandrum (CET). CET’s cricket team delivered a masterclass throughout the tournament, crushing every opponent with sheer dominance and ultimately claiming the championship trophy. A Glorious Start: Crushing Mohandas College CET kicked off their campaign in style with an easy win against Mohandas College. From the very first ball, it was clear who the superior team was. Captain Akshay led from the front, smashing 50 runs, while Rahul dismantled the opposition with a stunning 83-run knock. The bowling attack, spearheaded by Aheen’s phenomenal 4-wicket haul, ensured that Mohandas College never had a chance. CET wrapped up the match effortlessly, setting the tone for their victorious run. A Close Call Against Rajdhani College The second match against Rajdhani College was the only minor challenge CET faced in the tournament. While Rajdhani put up a fight, CET’s sheer resilience and never-give-up attitude saw them through. Once again, the team’s well-balanced batting and bowling attack ensured another win, pushing CET into the semifinals. Semifinal Showdown: Crushing Mar Basilius Engineering College If anyone thought CET might slow down, they were wrong. The semifinal against Mar Basilius Engineering College was another dominant performance. CET’s bowlers ripped through the opposition’s batting lineup, while the batsmen sealed the victory with ease. It was yet another one-sided affair, proving once again why CET was the undisputed powerhouse of the tournament. The Grand Finale: CET’s Commanding Comeback Against Muttathara Engineering College The much-anticipated final saw CET facing off against Muttathara Engineering College. Muttathara started strong, putting up a competitive total, but CET never panicked. Like true champions, they turned the game around brilliantly. Skipper Akshay stood tall till the very end, playing a sensible and heroic innings, guiding the team to a well-deserved victory. CET: The Undisputed Champions This victory reaffirms CET’s status as the best engineering college cricket team in Trivandrum. Every player contributed, showcasing a perfect blend of skill, determination, and teamwork. The team’s unmatched dominance in the tournament has set a new benchmark, and it’s clear that no team in the competition could match CET’s class. As the champions lifted the trophy, one thing was certain—CET didn’t just win the A-Zone Inter-College Cricket Tournament; they owned it!
